<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

वैशम्पायनाय पलिङ्गवे तित्तिरायोखायात्रेयाय पदकाराय, कौण्डिण्याय वृत्तिकाराय, सूत्रकारेभ्यः, सत्याषाढाय, प्रवचनकर्तृभ्य आचार्येभ्य, ऋषिभ्यो, वानप्रस्थेभ्य, ऊर्ध्वरेतोभ्य, एकपत्नीभ्य इति १
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

1. [^1]  For Vaiśampāyana, Paliṅgu, Tittira [sic], Ukha, Ātreya, the author of the Pada-text, Kauṇḍinya the author of the commentary, for the authors of the Sūtras, for Satyāṣāḍha (Hiraṇyakeśin), for the handers-down of the text, for the teachers, the Ṛṣis, the hermits dwelling in the woods, the chaste ones, for those who have only one wife.

[^1]:  20, 1. The Kāṇḍānukrama of the Ātreyī-śākhā, which has been printed by Professor Weber in his edition of the Taittirīya Saṃhitā, vol. ii, p. 356, shows that the dative Paliṅgave ought to be corrected to Paiṅgaye. The 'vṛttikāra' is there called not Kauṇḍinya, but Kuṇḍina.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

वैशम्पायनाय पलिङ्गवे तित्तिरायोखायात्रेयाय पदकाराय कौण्डिण्याय वृत्तिकाराय सूत्रकारेभ्यः सत्याषाढाय प्रवचनकर्तृभ्य आचार्येभ्य ऋषिभ्यो वानप्रस्थेभ्य ऊर्ध्वरेतोभ्य एकपत्नीभ्य इति १
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

यथास्वं पितृभ्यो मातामहेभ्यश्च कल्पयन्ति २
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2. They prepare (seats) each for his own fathers and maternal ancestors.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

यथास्वं पितृभ्यो मातामहेभ्यश्च कल्पयन्ति २
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अमुष्मै कल्पयाम्यमुष्मै कल्पयामि । इत्यासनेन ३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3. With (the words), 'For N.N. I prepare (a seat); for N.N. I prepare (a seat)' (he prepares) a seat.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अमुष्मै कल्पयाम्यमुष्मै कल्पयामि । इत्यासनेन ३
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अमुं तर्पयाम्यमुं तर्पयामि । इत्युदकेन ४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

4. With (the words), 'I satiate N.N.; I satiate N.N.' (he makes offerings of) water.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अमुं तर्पयाम्यमुं तर्पयामि । इत्युदकेन ४
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अमुष्मै नमोमुष्मै नमः । इति गन्धपुष्पधूपदीपैः ५
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

5. With (the words), 'Adoration to N: N.! Adoration to N.N.!' (he offers) perfumes, flowers, incense, and lamps.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अमुष्मै नमोमुष्मै नमः । इति गन्धपुष्पधूपदीपैः ५
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अमुष्मै स्वाहामुष्मै स्वाहा । इत्यन्नेन ६
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

6. With (the words), 'To N.N. svāhā! To N.N. svāhā!' (he offers) food.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अमुष्मै स्वाहामुष्मै स्वाहा । इत्यन्नेन ६
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अमुं तर्पयाम्यमुं तर्पयामि । इति फलोदकेन ७
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

7. With (the words), 'I satiate N.N.; I satiate N.N.' (he offers) water with fruits in it.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अमुं तर्पयाम्यमुं तर्पयामि । इति फलोदकेन ७
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अमुष्मै नमोमुष्मै नमः । इत्युपस्थाय ८
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

8. Having worshipped them with (the words), 'Adoration to N.N.! Adoration to N.N.!' - 

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अमुष्मै नमोमुष्मै नमः । इत्युपस्थाय ८
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अपरेण वेदिमग्निमुपसमाधाय व्याहृतिपर्यन्तं कृत्वा काण्डर्षीञ्जुहोति काण्डनामानि वा सावित्रीमृग्वेदं यजुर्वेदं सामवेदमथर्ववेदं सदसस्पतिमिति । हुत्वा प्रथमेनानुवाकेनाधीयते । काण्डादीन्वा सर्वान् । जयादि प्रतिपद्यते । स्विष्टकृदन्तं कृत्वा त्र्यहमेकाहं वा क्षम्य यथाध्यायमध्येतव्यमिति वदन्ति ९
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

9. [^2]  Having put wood on the fire to the west of the surface (on which he had performed the Tarpaṇa), and having performed the rites down to the Vyāhṛti oblations (&c., as above, chap. 18, 3-7).

[^2]:  There is only one difference between the text of this Sutra p. 246 and that of chap. 18, 3-7: instead of hutvā trīn āditoऽnuvākān adhīyate (18, 4) we read here, hutvā prathamenānuvākenādhīyate, which I believe must be translated, 'Having sacrificed with the first Anuvāka, they recite (that Anuvāka).' Mātṛdatta says, hutvā prathamottamānuvākam adhīyate.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अपरेण वेदिमग्निमुपसमाधाय व्याहृतिपर्यन्तं कृत्वा काण्डर्षीञ्जुहोति काण्डनामानि वा सावित्रीमृग्वेदं यजुर्वेदं सामवेदमथर्ववेदं सदसस्पतिमिति । हुत्वा प्रथमेनानुवाकेनाधीयते । काण्डादीन्वा सर्वान् । जयादि प्रतिपद्यते । स्विष्टकृदन्तं कृत्वा त्र्यहमेकाहं वा क्षम्य यथाध्यायमध्येतव्यमिति वदन्ति ९
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

काण्डात्काण्डात् । या शतेन । इति द्वाभ्यामुदकान्ते दूर्वा रोपयन्ति १०
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

10. With the two (verses), 'From joint to joint,' 'Thou who with a hundred' (Taitt. Saṃh. IV, 2, 9, 2) they plant Dūrvā grass at the shore of the water.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

काण्डात्काण्डात् । या शतेन । इति द्वाभ्यामुदकान्ते दूर्वा रोपयन्ति १०
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

उदधिमूर्मिमन्तं कृत्वा प्राचीमुदीचीं वा दिशमातमितोराजिं धावन्ति ११
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

11. They stir up waves in the water and run a race in an easterly or northerly direction until they lose their breath.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

उदधिमूर्मिमन्तं कृत्वा प्राचीमुदीचीं वा दिशमातमितोराजिं धावन्ति ११
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

प्रत्येत्यापूपैः सक्तुभिरोदनेनेति ब्राह्मणांस्तर्पयन्ति १२
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

12. When they have returned (from that race? or when they have returned from the whole ceremony to the village?) they offer cakes, coarsely ground grain, and boiled rice to the Brāhmaṇas.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

प्रत्येत्यापूपैः सक्तुभिरोदनेनेति ब्राह्मणांस्तर्पयन्ति १२
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

एवं पारायणसमाप्तौ दूर्वारोपणोदधिधावनवर्जम् १३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

13. [^3]  The same (rites are repeated) when they have finished the study of the whole Veda, with the exception of the planting of Dūrvā grass, of (stirring up) the water, and of the race.

[^3]:  See Sūtras 10 and 11.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

एवं पारायणसमाप्तौ दूर्वारोपणोदधिधावनवर्जम् १३
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

नित्यमेवाद्भिर्देवानृषीन्पितॄंश्च तर्पयन्ति तर्पयन्ति १४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

14. Thus they satiate daily (after the Brahmayajña) the gods, the Ṛṣis, and the Fathers with water; they satiate them with water.

End of the Hiraṇyakeśi-sūtra.

</details>

</details>
