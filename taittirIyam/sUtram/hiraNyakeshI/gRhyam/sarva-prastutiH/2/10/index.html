Then he sacrifices with their names: ‘To N.N. svadh─Б! Adoration! To N.N. svadh─Б! Adoration!’

‘Wherein my mother has done amiss, abandoning her duty (towards her husband), may my father take that sperm as his own; may another one fall off from the mother. Svadh─Б! Adoration!’

In the same way a second and a third verse with the alteration of the Mantra, ‘Wherein my grandmother,’ ‘Wherein my great-grandmother.’
