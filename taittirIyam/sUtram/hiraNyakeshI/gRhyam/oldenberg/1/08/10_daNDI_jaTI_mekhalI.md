10. [^10]  He wears a staff, has his hair tied in one knot, and wears a girdle,


[^10]:  10, 11. Comp. Āpastamba I, I, 2, 31. 32. Mātṛdatta has received into his explanation of the eleventh Sūtra the words, 'he should shave the rest of the hair,' which in the Āpastambīya-sūtra are found in the text.
