4. [^3]  He sacrifices with the Vyāhṛtis, 'Bhūḥ! Bhuvaḥ! Suvaḥ!' - with the single (three Vyāhṛtis) and with (the three) together.


[^3]:  Śāṅkhāyana I, 12, 12. 13; Gobhila I, 9, 27. As to suvaḥ, the spelling of the Taittirīyas for svaḥ, see Indische Studien, XIII, 105.
