5. [^4]  (The Mantras for the two chief oblations are), the (verse), 'Life-giving, Agni!' (Taitt. Brāhmaṇa I, 2, 1, 11), (and),


[^4]:  5, 6. In the second Mantra we should read vṛṇāno instead of gṛṇāno; comp. Atharva-veda II, 13, 1. As to the Mantras that follow, comp. Pāraskara I, 2, 8; Taittirīya Āraṇyaka IV, 20, 3. - Regarding the Mantra tvam Agne ayāsi (sic), comp. Taitt. Brāh. II, 4, 1, 9; Āśvalāyana-Śrauta-sūtra I, 11, 13; Kātyāyana-Śrauta-sūtra XXV, 1, 11; Indische Studien, XV, 125.

'Life-giving, O god, choosing long life, thou whose face is full of ghee, whose back is full of ghee, Agni, drinking ghee, the noble ambrosia that comes from the cow, lead this (boy) to old age, as a father (leads) his son. Svāhā!'
