1. [^1]  With (the formula), 'The milk of Virāj art thou. May the milk of Padyā Virāj (dwell) in me' - (the guest) touches the hands of the person that washes his feet, and then he touches himself with (the formula), 'May in me dwell brilliancy, energy, strength, life, renown, splendour, glory, power!'


[^1]:  13, 1. Comp. Śāṅkhāyana III, 7, 5, &c.
