16. [^10]  After those (Brāhmaṇas) have eaten, (the host) orders blameless (?) food to be brought to him (i.e. to the guest).


[^10]:  The meaning of anusaṃvṛjinam (comp. above, I, 4, 12, 15) is uncertain. See the commentary, p. 120 of Dr. Kirste's edition.
