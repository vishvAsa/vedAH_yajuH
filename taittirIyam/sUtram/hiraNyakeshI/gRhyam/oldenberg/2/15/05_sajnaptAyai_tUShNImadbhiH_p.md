5. [^3]  After it has been killed, he silently 'strengthens' its sense-organs (by touching them) with water, and silently takes out the omentum, the heart, and the kidneys.


[^3]:  On the 'strengthening' of the sense-organs of an immolated victim, comp. Āpastamba-Śrauta-sūtra VII, 18, 6 seq. Schwab, Thieropfer, 110. - On matasne, see Indische Studien, IX, 248; Schwab 127.
