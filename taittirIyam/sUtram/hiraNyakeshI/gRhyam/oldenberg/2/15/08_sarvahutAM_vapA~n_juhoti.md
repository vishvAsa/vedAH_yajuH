8. [^4]  He sacrifices the omentum entirely. The other parts (Sūtra 5) he should offer to the Brāhmaṇas and should feed them (with those parts of the cow).


[^4]:  Possibly the reading of the Udīcyas indicated by Mātṛdatta, vyākṛtya instead of upākṛtya, is correct. The translation would be, 'With the rest, distributing it, &c.'
