4. Then he washes his hands and touches the earth with (the formulas), 'In power I establish myself, in royalty. Among the horses I establish myself, among the cows. In the limbs I establish myself, in the self. In the Prāṇas I establish myself, in prosperity. In Heaven and Earth I establish myself, in sacrifice.

'May the three times eleven gods, the thirty-three, the gracious ones, whose Purohita is Bṛhaspati, on the impulse of the god Savitṛ - may the gods with (all) the gods give me bliss!'
