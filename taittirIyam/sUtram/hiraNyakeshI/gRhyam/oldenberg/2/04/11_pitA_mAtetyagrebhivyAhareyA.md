11. [^3]  Let the father and the mother pronounce (that name) first. For it is understood, 'My name first, O Jātavedas.'


[^3]:  The verse beginning with 'My name,' &c., contains the words, 'which my father and my mother have given me in the beginning' (pitā mātā ca dadhatur yad agre).
