<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

आपस्तम्बानुयायित्वम्  
अभिवादयतां मृषा।  
त्रपां हर्तुं सताम् भूयाद्  
ग्रन्थोऽयं विष्णु-कारितः॥
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

May this book, motivated by Vishnu,  
serve to remove the embarrassment of those virtuous people  
who [currently] greet [elders]  
dishonestly mentioning their followership of the Apastamba school  
(not having yet read his texts).
</details>
