02 कृष्णो ऽस्याखरेष्ठो देव ...{Loading}...

पुरस्तात् प्रत्यञ्चं ग्रन्थिम् उपगूहति, पश्चात्प्राञ्चं वा ।

थिते
  1. With kr̥ṣṇo’syākhareṣṭhaḥ………1 he ties up (the fuel by means of a cord). He conceals the knot either from east to west or from west to east.2 He keeps it down not directly upon the ground (but on some grass spread on the ground).3

थिते
