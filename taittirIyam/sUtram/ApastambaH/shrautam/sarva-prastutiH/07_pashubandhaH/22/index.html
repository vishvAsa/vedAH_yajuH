06 हृदयञ् जिह्वा वक्षो ...{Loading}...

हृदयं जिह्वा वक्षो यकृद्वृक्यौ सव्यं दोरुभे पार्श्वे दक्षिणा श्रोणिर्गुदतृतीयमिति दैवतानि । दक्षिणं दोः सव्या श्रोणिर्गुदतृतीयमिति सौविष्टकृतानि । क्लोमानं लीहानं पुरीततं वनिष्ठुमध्यूध्नीं मेदो जाघनीमित्युद्धरति ६

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते
  1. Heart, tongue, sternum, liver, the two kidneys, left arm (=fore-foot),1 both the two thoracic walls, the right buttock, one third of the rectum,-(these are the limbs which are to be offered) to deities (as the chief offerings),2 the right arm (=fore foot), the left buttock, and one-third of the rectum-these are the limbs to be offered to Agni Sviṣṭakr̥t; the pancreas, spleen, pericardium, inside of the larger intestine, testis with penis, fat and the tail3-these he extracts.
