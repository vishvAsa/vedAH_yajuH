16. With the two (i.e. a verse and a formula viz.) tvaṁ soma tanūkr̥dbhyaḥ...[^1] and juṣāṇaḥ...[^2] he offers half[^3] ghee (in the additional ladle) in the Gārhapatya fire.[^4]

[^2]: TS I.3.4.b.
