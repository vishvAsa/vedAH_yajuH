थिते

One performs the Iṣṭis in the manner of Soma-pressings every day for one year: (Thus one should offer a sacrificial bread) prepared on eight potsherds to Agni (at the time of the morning pressing);… prepared on eleven potsherds to Indra (at the time of the midday pressing);… prepared on twelve potsherds to Viśvedevas (at the time of the third pressing).
