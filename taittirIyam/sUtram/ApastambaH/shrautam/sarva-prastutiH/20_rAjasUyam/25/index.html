02 पञ्चबिलस्य चरोर्विज्ञायते आज्य ...{Loading}...

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते
  1. It is knuwn about the five-holed rice-pap as follows :(in a pot having five cavities) rice-pap in ghee for Agni in the eastern cavity, (rice-pap) in curds, for Indra in the southern (cavity), (rice-pap) in cooked milk or in cooked milk mixed with fresh butter for the Viśvedevas in the western (cavity), (rice-pap) in water for Mitra-Varuṇa in the northern (cavity), (rice-pap) in milk for Br̥haspati in the central (cavity) (should be cooked and then offered).1
