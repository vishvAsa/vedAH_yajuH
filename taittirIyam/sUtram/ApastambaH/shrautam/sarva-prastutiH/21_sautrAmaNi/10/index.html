03 आकाशाद्गृह्णीयादित्येकम् तार्तीयसवनिकस्य सोमस्य ...{Loading}...

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते
  1. (There is ) one (view according to which) he takes it from the sky; (there is) another (view according to which) he should let aside as much of the Soma used for the third pressing as he may think to be enough for (filling) one group of Camasas along with (the performance of a) scoop.
