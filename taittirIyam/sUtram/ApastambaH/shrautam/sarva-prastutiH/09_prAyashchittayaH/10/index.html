सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते
  1. If (all the fires get extinguished) when wind is still, in that case having churned out fire, then having prepared the sacrificial place (Vihāra), having sat dawn to the west of the Āhavanīya one should oneself drink the Agnihotra milk1. It is known from a Brāhamṇa-text that this is the substitute of the Agnihotra-offering.
