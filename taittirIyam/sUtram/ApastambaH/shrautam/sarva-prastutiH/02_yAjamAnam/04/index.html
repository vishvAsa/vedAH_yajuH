02 घृताचीरेताग्निर्वो ह्वयति देवयज्याया ...{Loading}...

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते
  1. With ghr̥tācīreta…1 having taken up the ladles, with pratyuṣṭaṁ rakṣaḥ pratyuṣṭā arātayaḥ2 having heated them either on the Āhavanīya or gārhapatya with aniṣitā stha sapatnakayiṇiḥ3 having addressed them, having either divided the tips of the Veda in parts according to the number of ladles or not, he cleanses the ladles by means of them (tips) holding them (the ladles) with their openings turned upwards, with their points either to the east or to the north.
