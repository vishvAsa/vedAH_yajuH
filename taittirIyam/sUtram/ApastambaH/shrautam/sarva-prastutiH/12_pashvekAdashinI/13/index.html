थिते
  1. Having stituated himself towards the north of the fire, having brought (the cup) towards the northern joint3 of the enclosing-sticks, he offers (the libation of the Soma-juice) in the northern part4 of the fire.

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते
  1. Having poured a little less1 than the entire quantity of the remnant (Soma-juice)2 into the Āgrayaṇa-vessel, having placed the non-empty cup in its place3 with eṣa te yonirapānāya tvā4, (he places) the Upāṁśusavana-pressing-stone with its mouth pointing towards the south and (itself) touching the cups5 between them (i.e. Upāṁśu-cup and Antaryāma-cup) with vyānāya tvā.6
