थिते

07 उपांशुङ् गृहीत्वामुष्य त्वा ...{Loading}...

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते

Having taken the Upāṁśu (-scoop), with amuṣya tvā prāṇe sādayāmi1 having deposited (the cup containing the scoop on the mound), with devasya tvā savituḥ prasave… having taken it2, having covered (the cup) by means of his hand with amuṣya tvā prāṇamapidadhāmi,3 he should say: amuṁ jahyatha tvā hoṣyāmi.4
