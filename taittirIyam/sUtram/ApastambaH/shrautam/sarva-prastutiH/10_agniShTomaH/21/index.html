06 याः कृतायां वेद्याञ् ...{Loading}...

याः कृतायां वेद्यां चोद्यन्ते सैव तासां वेदिः । याः स्तीर्णे बर्हिषि तदेव तासां बर्हिः ६

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
थिते
  1. For those offerings (constituting a part of the son sacrifice), which are prescribed (to be performed) after the Vedi (altar) for an earlier offering has been already prepared, the same (old) Vedi should be used. For those offerings (which are prescribed to be performed) after the sacrificial grass has been already scattered, the same sacrificial grass should be used.
