5. Having stood near fires praising them with Virāṭkrama (verses) as described earlier,[^1] having taken food, when he is now about go on journey, he says: “Do you add fuel to the fires".[^2]  

[^1]: See V.18.2.  

[^2]: Cf. TB I.1.10.4.
