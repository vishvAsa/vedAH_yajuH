4. Having spread an underlayer (of ghee in the Prāśitra vessel), he does not pour (ghee over the Prāśitra-portion) or (he does this) in a reverse manner (i.e. he does spread an underlayer, but pours ghee upon the Prāśitra-portion) or he spreads an underlayer of ghee and (also) pours (ghee upon the portions).
