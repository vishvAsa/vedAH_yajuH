2. Having moistened the points (of the grass) in the ladle[^1] he moistens the roots (of the grass in it)[^2].  

[^1]: In the water in the Agnihotrahavaṇī i.e. the ladle with which the Agnihotra-libation is offered.  

[^2]: Cf. SB I.3.3.4.
