2. (The post should have) eight sides,[^1] thinner (from the root-part) towards the top, with the side which will stand facing the fire well-marked (in advance), neither (too) broad nor (too) thin.[^2]  


[^1]: Cf. TS VI.3.3.6.  

[^2]: Cf. MS III.9.4.
