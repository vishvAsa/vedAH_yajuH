21. The beginning of the formula should be modified as vad gharmaḥ paryavartayat... [^1]; then agnistigmena... is the same.[^2]  


[^1]: Instead of r̥tameva parameṣṭhī... (see VIII.4.2), this verse (TB I.5.5.2.3) should be used.
