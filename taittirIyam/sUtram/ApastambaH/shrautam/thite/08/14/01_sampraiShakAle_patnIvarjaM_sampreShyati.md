1. At the time of giving orders[^1] the Adhvaryu gives orders excluding one connected with the wife of the sacrificer.[^2]  


[^1]: See II.3.11.  

[^2]: Thus he omits the words patniṁ saṁnahya "(having girdled the wife of the sacrificer)."
