16. With punarūrjā...[^1] and with saha rayyā...[^2] (the Adhvaryu) offers libations on both the sides[^3] of the sacrificial bread[^4].  

[^1-2]: TS I.5.3.i-k.  

[^3]: i.e. one libation before the offering of the sacrificial bread and one after.


[^4]: Cf. TS I.5.4.4.
