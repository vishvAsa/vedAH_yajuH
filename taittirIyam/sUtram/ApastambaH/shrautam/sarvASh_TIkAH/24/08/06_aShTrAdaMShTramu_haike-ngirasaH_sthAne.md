6. According to some there should be Aṣṭādaṁṣṭra instead of Aṅgiras. (Then the Hotr̥ says:) O Āṣṭadaṁṣṭra, Vairupa, Pārṣadaśva. (The Adhvaryu says:) In the manner of Pr̥ṣadaśva, Virūpa, Aṣṭādaṁṣṭra.
