<details><summary>Müller</summary>

Sacrificial acts intended for the gods, should be performed by the priest towards the east or towards the north, after he has placed the Brahmanic cord over the left and under the right arm (yajñopavītin), and turning towards the right.
</details>

<details><summary>थिते</summary>

15. Carrying the sacrificial garment on the left shoulder and under the right armpit, one should carry out the activities connected to gods, ending in the east or ending in the north and one goes always in a clock-wise manner.  
</details>
