<details><summary>Müller</summary>

Sacrificial acts intended for the Fathers should be performed by the priest towards the south, after he has placed the Brahmanic cord over the right and under the left arm (prācīnāvītin), and turning towards the left.
</details>

<details><summary>थिते</summary>

16. Wearing one's sacrificial garment on the right shoulder and under the left armpit one should carry out the activities connected with ancestors, ending in the south; and (one goes) in an anti-clock-wise manner.  
</details>
