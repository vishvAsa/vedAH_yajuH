16. One should however build (the fire-altar) of the size of seven men (at the time of the first building). The fire-altar building of the size of seven men is indeed the basic one and after that (the size of the fire-altar-building should be) more by one (man) (in each successive occasion)"[^1].  

[^1]: Cp. ŚB X.2.3.17-18.
