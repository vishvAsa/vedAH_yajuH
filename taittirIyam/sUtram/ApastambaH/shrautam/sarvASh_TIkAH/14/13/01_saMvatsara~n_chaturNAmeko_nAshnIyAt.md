<details><summary>थिते</summary>

1. In connection with one who is studying the Caturhotr̥. formulae[^1] it is known (from a Brāhmaṇa-text): “One should not eat for a year, being one of the four. This is the vow."[^2]  

[^1]: TĀ III.2.  

[^2]: MS I.9.6.
