15. From the time of the depositing of the Dhruva-scoop upto that of the pouring of it,[^1] the sacrificer does not urinate.[^2]  

[^1]: Thus from XII.16.3 uplo XIII. 16.1.  

[^2]: Cf. KS XXVIII. 1; cf. ŚB IV.2.4.8.
