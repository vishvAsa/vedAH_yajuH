<details><summary>थिते</summary>

9. Having offered the Āgneya (scoop) with agne tejasvin... the Pratiprasthātr̥ addresses it with tejovid asi... Having offered the Aindra (-scoop) with indraujasvin... the Neṣṭr̥ addresses it
with ojovidasi... having offered the Saurya (-scoop) with surya bhrājasvin... the Unnetr̥ addresses it with suvarvidasi...[^1]  

[^1]: All the formulae TS III.3.1.a-f.
