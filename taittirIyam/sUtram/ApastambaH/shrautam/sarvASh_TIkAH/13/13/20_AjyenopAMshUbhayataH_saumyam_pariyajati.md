20. Reciting verses inaudibly[^1] he offers ghee on both the sides[^2] of the (offering of the rice-pap) for Soma or only one of the sides.[^3]   


[^1]: Cf. TS. VI.6.7.3.

[^3]: Cf. TS. VI.6.7.3; MS IV.7.3.
