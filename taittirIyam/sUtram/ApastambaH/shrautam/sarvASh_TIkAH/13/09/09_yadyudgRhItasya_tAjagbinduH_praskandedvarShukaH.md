9. From the (Upāṁśusavana-stone) if a drop falls quickly (then one may suppose that) there will be rain (very soon); if it (falls) after a long time, there will be no rain.[^1]  

[^1]: Cf. TS V1.5.6.5; cp. KS XXVIII.6. 7
