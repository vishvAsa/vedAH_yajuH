1. With pr̥thivyāḥ sampr̥caḥ pāhi[^1] (the Adhvaryu) keeps down (the Prastara) not (directly) upon the ground.[^2]:  

[^1]: TS I.1.2.k.  

[^2]: Thus he keeps some grass on the ground and on it he keeps down the Prastara.
