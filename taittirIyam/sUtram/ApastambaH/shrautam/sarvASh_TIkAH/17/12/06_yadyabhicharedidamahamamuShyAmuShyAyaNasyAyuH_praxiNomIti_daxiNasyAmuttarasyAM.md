6. If he wants to practise black magic (against the enemy) idamahamamuṣyāmuṣyāyaṇasya... he should destroy the jar on the southern or northern corner.[^1]  

[^1]: Cf. MS III.3.5; KS XXI.7. According MS and KS this is to be done on the south-western or north-western corner.
