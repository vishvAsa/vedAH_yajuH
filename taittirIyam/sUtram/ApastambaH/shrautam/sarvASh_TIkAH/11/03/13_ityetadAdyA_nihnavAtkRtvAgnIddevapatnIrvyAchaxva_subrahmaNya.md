13. Having performed the ritual beginning with the question, “O Āgnīdhra, is the water boiling?” upto the amends,[^1] (he orders), “O Agnīdhra, do you recite the Devapatnī-formulae; O Subrahmaṇya, do you call the Subrahmaṇyā.[^2]  


[^1]: i.e. the ritual mentioned in XI.1.8-12.  

[^2]: See for this order GB II.2.9.
