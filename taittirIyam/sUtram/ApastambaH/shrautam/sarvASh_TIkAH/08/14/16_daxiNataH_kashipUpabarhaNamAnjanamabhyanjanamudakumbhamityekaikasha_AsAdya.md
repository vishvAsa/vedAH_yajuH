16. Having placed a kushion and a pillow, collyrium and ointment, and a waterjar[^1] one-by-one to the south of the oblations, then having kept down the brush (Veda) he starts the work of Sāmidhenī verses.  


[^1]: Cf. TB I.6.8.9; cp. also I.8.2.
