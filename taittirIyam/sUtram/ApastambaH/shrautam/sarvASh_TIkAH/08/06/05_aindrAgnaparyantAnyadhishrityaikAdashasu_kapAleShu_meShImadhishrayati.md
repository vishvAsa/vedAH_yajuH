5. After the Adhvaryu has placed the offering materials, upto the one for Indra and Agni, on the fire, (the Pratiprasthātr̥) should place (the figure) of ewe on the eleven potsherds; (the Adhvaryu places) the (figure of) ram on eight potsherds.
