4. Now according to some h(ritualists)[^1] the sacrificer who desires cattle should perform Vaiśvadeva-parvan instead of the other Parvans until he gets one thousand cattle. Then he should perform the other (Parvans) at their own times.  


[^1]: KS XXXVI.3; MS I. 10.7;
