3. Having called the Brahman, with brahman pravarāyāśrāvayiṣyāmi he makes the Āgnīdhra to say astu śrauṣaṭ with the words āśrāvaya,[^1] or ośrāvaya or with śrāvaya or with oṁ śrāvaya.  

[^1]: Cf. TS I.6.11.1.
