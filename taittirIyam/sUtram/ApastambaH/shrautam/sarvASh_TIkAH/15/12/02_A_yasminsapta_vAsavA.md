<details><summary>थिते</summary>

2. Having come to know (of the Hotr̥ reciting) ā yasmin sapta vāsavā..., having carried round the Pravargya-utensils along the front of the Āhavanīya-fire, he deposits them on the emperor's throne with svāhā tvā sūryasya rasmibhyaḥ[^2] in the 
morning; svāhā tvā nakṣatrebhyaḥ[^3] in the evening.  

[^1]: Cf. TĀ V.8.13.  

[^2-3]: TĀ IV.10.12.
