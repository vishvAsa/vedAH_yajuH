7. With devastvā savitodvapatu...[^1] having taken it out (of the fire), with apadyamānaḥ pr̥thivyām...[^2] having put it down towards the north on the sand,[^3] with sūryasya tvā cakṣuṣānvikṣed he looks at it.[^4]  

[^1]: TĀ IV.3.2(5).  

[^2]: TĀ IV.3.2(6).  

[^3]: See VI.5.11.  

[^4]: TĀ XVI.3.2(7).  

[^5]: Cf. TĀ V.3.7.
