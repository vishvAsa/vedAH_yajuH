<details><summary>थिते</summary>

8. If (the Gharma-cow) sits on a dirty thing or on something which is impure from the point of view of the sacrifice, he should offer a libation (of ghee) with a verse addressed to Agni;[^1] with a verse addressed to Agni and Varuṇa,[^2] according to some ritualists.  

[^1]: TS I.1.14.1.  

[^2]: TS II.5.12.w.
