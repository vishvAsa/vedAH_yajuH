<details><summary>थिते</summary>

5. Having taken out one hundred and four golden dice with audbhidyaṁ rājñaḥ[^1] having won (the game) he gives five dice to the king with diśobhyayaṁ rājābhūt.[^2]  

[^1]: Cp. MS IV.4.6.  

[^2]: Cf. TB I.7.10.5.
