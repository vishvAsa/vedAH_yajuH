<details><summary>थिते</summary>

3. Having placed[^1] these (gift materials) the king sends them to the opponent kings.  

[^1]: I sopport the reading abhyavasthāpya. Caland accepts the reading abhyavasnāpya (having bathed) as correct and also quotes Tālavr̥ntavāsin's commentary. The commentor's interpretation as well as that of Caland appears to be artificial and based upon the wrong reading. There is no occasion to bathe the gift here.
