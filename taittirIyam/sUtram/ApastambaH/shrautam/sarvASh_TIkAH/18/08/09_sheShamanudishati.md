9. The remaining (cows) he assigns.[^1]   

[^1]: When a big number cows are to be given the procedure may take a long time. Therefore, only a few cow may bé "led” in their proper time i.e should be led (given) literally and others are to be simply assigned to the priests. Thus all the cows need not be ritually led.
