13. Therefore at the time when they should perform the ritual of the omenta of the earlier animals, at the same time(they should perform the ritual of the omenta) of the other (animals) and when (they perform the ritual of the) (earlier animals, at that time) with the oblations of them (the limbs of the other animals).[^1]   

[^1]: Cf. ŚB V.1.3.13-14.
