16. From every direction (four) Vaiśyas or the (four) chief priests throw towards him (Sacrificer), sachets of Aśvattha (leaves)[^1] containing ash[^2] or salty clay or both, after having bound them on long bamboo sticks.  

[^1]: Cf. ŚB V.2.1.17.  

[^2]: See TB I.3.7.6.  
</details>
