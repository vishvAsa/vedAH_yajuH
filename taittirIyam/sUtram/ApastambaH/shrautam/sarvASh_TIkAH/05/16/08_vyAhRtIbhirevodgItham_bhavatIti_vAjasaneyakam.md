8. The Vājasaneyaka (opinion is that the work of) Udgītha (singing) is done by the sacred utterances (Vyāhr̥tīs) them selves.[^1]  

[^1]: This opinion is not found in either ŚB or Kātyāśs. See, however, ŚB II.1.4.14, 25.
