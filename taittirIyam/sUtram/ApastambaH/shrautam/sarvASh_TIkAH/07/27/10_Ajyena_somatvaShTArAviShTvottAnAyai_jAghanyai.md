10. Having made libations of ghee to Soma and Tvaṣṭr̥, from the non-hairy part of the tail (the Adhvaryu) cuts (two) portions for the wives of the gods, from the lower hairy part for Agni Gr̥hapati[^1].  


[^1]: Cf. ŚB III.8.5.6-7.
