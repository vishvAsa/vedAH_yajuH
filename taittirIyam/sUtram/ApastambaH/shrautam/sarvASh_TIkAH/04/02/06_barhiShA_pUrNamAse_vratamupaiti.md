6. At (the time of bringing of the) Barhis (sacred grass) (the sacrificer) takes vow on the Full-moon-day; after the calves have been removed (from their mother-cows he takes the vow) on the New-moon-day.[^1]  

[^1]: Cf. TS I.6.7.2.
