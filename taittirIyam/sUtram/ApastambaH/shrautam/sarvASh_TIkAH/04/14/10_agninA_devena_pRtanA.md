10. (The formulae beginning with) agninā devena pr̥tanā jayāmi[^1] (are called) Viṣṇvatikrama(-formulae). The formulae beginning with ye devā yajñahanaḥ[^2] are called Atīmokṣa-formulae.  


[^1]: TS III.5.3.ac.  

[^2]: TS III.5.4.a-t.
