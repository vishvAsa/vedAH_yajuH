<details><summary>थिते</summary>

1. In the second (call) (the Adhvaryu) orders, “O Brahman do you recite the offering verse”; in the third, “O Potr̥ do you recite the offering verse”; in the fourth, “O Neṣṭr̥, do you recite the offering verse"; in the fifth, “O Agnīdh, do you recite the offering verse.”
