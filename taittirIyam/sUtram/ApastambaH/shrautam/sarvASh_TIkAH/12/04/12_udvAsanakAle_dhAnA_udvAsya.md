<details><summary>थिते</summary>

12. At the time of taking the oblations away (from the fire),[^1] having taken away the fried grains, having divided into two parts with the formula to be used for dividing[^2] he mixes the half of them with ghee. In the procedure of making flour[^3] he makes the (other) half, to be coarse flour.   

[^1]: See II.10.6.  

[^2]: See I.24.5.  

[^3]: See I.21.5-7.
