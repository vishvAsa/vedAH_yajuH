11. Or rather after (the Adhvaryu) has placed the Sukra cup containing the remnant (of the Soma-juice) on its place (on the Khara-mound),[^1] (the Pratiprasthātr̥ also keeps some remnants of Soma-juice in the Manthin-cup and) having drawn out a burning coal from the northern part of the Āhavanīya) outside the enclosing sticks, he offers a libation of the remnants from the Manthin-cup (on the burning coal) with eṣa te rudra bhāgaḥ...[^2]  

[^1]: See XII.28.4.  

[^2]: TS III.1.9.f.
</details>
