15. At the time when the middle praise-verse (is being sung) he mutters the Anvāroha[^1] (-formula) viz. Śyeno'si gāyatracchandāḥ...[^2]  

[^1]: "Climbing". This formula is believed to be helping the sacrificer to "climb" upon the Pavamāna-lauds. cf. TS III.2.1.1.  

[^2]: TS III.2.1.1.
