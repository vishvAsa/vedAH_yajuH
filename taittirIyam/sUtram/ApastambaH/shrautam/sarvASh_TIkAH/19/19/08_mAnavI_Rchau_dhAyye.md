8. When it is said, “One should make two Manu-verses as “dhāyyās” (verses to be inserted)[^1] (in the Sāmidhenī-verse)" (it means that) two out of (the three) which begin with maksu devavatāḥ[^2] (are to be used).  

[^1]: TS II.2.10.2.  

[^2]: TS IL.8.22.1-m.
