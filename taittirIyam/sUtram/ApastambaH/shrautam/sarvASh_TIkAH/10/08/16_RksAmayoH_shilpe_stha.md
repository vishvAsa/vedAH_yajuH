16. With rkṣāmayoḥ śilpe stha[^1] (the sacrificer) should touch[^2] the two lines on the skin of black antelope. According to some (ritualists) he should hold them.[^3]  


[^1]: TS I.2.2.d.  

[^2]: KS XXIII.3; MS III.6.6.

[^3]: Cf. ŚB III.2.1.5.
