4. Having said, “I purchase your Soma which is strong and juicy"[^1] (the Adhvaryu says to him). “Let me purchase it for a sixteenth part[^2] (of a cow)."[^3]  


[^1]: TS I.2.7.a.a.  


[^2]: i.e. the lower part of a cow's leg.  


[^3]: TS VI.1.10.1.
