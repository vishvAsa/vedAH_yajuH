10. Having placed (the oblation-substance) meant for the Ātithyā-iṣṭi (on the altar)[^1] with the Caturhotr̥-formulae,[^2] he recites the Sambhārayajus (-formulae).[^3]  


[^1]: Cp. II.11.5.  

[^2]: TA III.2.  

[^3]: See X.3.5-6. For this Sūtra cf. TB II.2.2.6.
