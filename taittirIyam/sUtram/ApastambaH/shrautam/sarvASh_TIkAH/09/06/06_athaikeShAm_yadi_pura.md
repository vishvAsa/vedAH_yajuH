6. Now according to some: if the (Agnihotra-milk) kept in front but not offered spills, one should offer the same (milk) of whatever quantity it is; then having milked another cow one should offer the Agnihotra; then one should make a libation of ghee (with a verse to Varuṇa and recite the verse) to Varuṇa in the same manner.[^1]  


[^1]: See for details IX.6.1.
