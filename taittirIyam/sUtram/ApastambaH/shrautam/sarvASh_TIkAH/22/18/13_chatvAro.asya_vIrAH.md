13. It has been said in a Brāhmana-text[^1]: “Four heroic sons are born in his family: a good Hotr̥ priest, (a good Udgātr̥ priest, a good Adhvaryu priest and one good in assembly).[^1]   

[^1]: See TS VII.1.8.1.
