<details><summary>Bühler</summary>

17. Nor in a forest, if (a corpse or Cāṇḍāla) is within sight.
</details>

<details><summary>हरदत्त-टीका</summary>

## सूत्रम्
सन्दर्शने चाऽरण्ये ॥ १७ ॥  
## टिप्पनी
