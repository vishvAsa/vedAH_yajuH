<details><summary>Bühler</summary>

15-16. He may likewise study or teach in the presence of his teacher, if (the latter) has addressed him (saying), 'Ho, study! (or, Ho, teach!)' [^10] 


[^10]:  -16. Manu II, 73.
</details>
