<details><summary>Bühler</summary>

22. Or he is freed (from his sin), if (after the twelve years' penance) he bathes (with the priests) at the end of a horse-sacrifice. [^9] 


[^9]:  Manu XI, 83; Weber, Ind. Stud. X, 67.
</details>
