<details><summary>Bühler</summary>

18. (If he performs it) on the eleventh day (its reward is) black iron, tin, and lead.
</details>

## टिप्पनी
कृष्णमयः कृष्णायसम् । त्रपुसीसे लोहविशेषौ ॥ १७ ॥
