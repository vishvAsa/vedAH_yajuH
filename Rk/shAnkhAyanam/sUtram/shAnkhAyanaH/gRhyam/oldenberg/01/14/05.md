5 [^2] . (The Ācārya?) makes (them) step forward in a north-eastern direction seven steps (with the words),


[^2]:  5, 7. According to Nārāyaṇa it is the teacher who makes them walk the seven steps; the Paddhati says that the bridegroom or the Ācārya causes her to do so. Comp. Pāraskara I, 8, 1; Āśvalāyana I, 7, 19, &c.
