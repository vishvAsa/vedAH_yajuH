6 [^4] . The teacher having abstained through one day and one night, from sexual intercourse and from eating flesh


[^4]:  The pleonasm brahmacaryam upetya . . . brahmacārī should be removed by expunging brahmacārī, which is omitted in the Śāmbavya text. Comp. chap. 12, Sūtra 8; VI, 1, 2.
