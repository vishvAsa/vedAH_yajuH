12. If he chooses to let it loose, (he murmurs), 'This cow will become a milch cow.

'The mother of the Rudras, the daughter of the Vasus, the sister of the Ādityas, the navel of immortality. To the people who understand me, I say, "Do not kill the guiltless cow, which is Aditi."

'Let it drink water! Let it eat grass'

(And) gives order (to the people), 'Om! Let it loose.'
