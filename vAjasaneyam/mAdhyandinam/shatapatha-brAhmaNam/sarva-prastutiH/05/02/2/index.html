सायणः

अथ नैवारचरुप्रचारं विधत्ते- बार्हस्पत्येनेति 1 । प्रधाननैवाराहुतेरनन्तरं कर्त्तव्यं वाजप्रसवोयहोमं विधातुं कालं विधत्ते- तस्यानिष्ट एवेति । ‘तस्य’ नैवारस्य स्विष्टकृत् ‘अनिष्टः’ अनिष्टकृद् ‘भवति’ प्रधानयागादूर्ध्वं स्विष्टकृतः प्रागित्यर्थः । होमार्थमन्नसम्भरणं विधत्ते- अथास्मा इति । ‘अस्मै’ यजमानाय अभिषेकार्थम् ‘अन्नं सम्भरेत्’ ॥ १ ॥

Eggeling
  1. He now proceeds with the Bārhaspatya pap. Its svishṭakr̥t remains yet unoffered, when he (the Adhvaryu) brings 2 him (the Sacrificer) some food; for he who offers the Vājapeya wins food, vājapeya being the same as anna-peya: thus whatever food he (the Sacrificer) has thereby gained, that he (the Adhvaryu) now brings to him.

Eggeling
  1. Now some bring seventeen kinds of food,

saying, ‘Seventeenfold is Prajāpati.’ But let him not do so: surely all the food is not appropriated to Prajāpati 5, and, compared to him, what is man that he should appropriate to himself all food? Hence, while bringing every kind of food that occurs to him, let him not bring of some one (particular) kind of food.

Eggeling
  1. And whatever food he does trot bring to him, let him (the Sacrificer) forswear 8 that, and not eat of it as long as he lives: thus he does not go to the end, thus he lives long. Of all that food brought together he offers the (seven) Vāja-prasavanīya 9 oblations, cutting out (pieces) with the dipping-spoon. Thus to whatever deities he is now offering, they give an impulse to him, and impelled by them he

wins: therefore he offers the Vājaprasavanīya oblations.
