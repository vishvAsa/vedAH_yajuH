##  ०१
<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

सो᳘मो वै रा᳘जा यज्ञः᳘ प्रजा᳘पतिः ॥  
त᳘स्यैता᳘स्तॗन्वो या᳘ एता᳘ देव᳘ता या᳘ एता आ᳘हुतीर्जुहो᳘ति ॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

1. Verily, Prajāpati, the sacrifice, is King Soma; and these deities to whom he offers, and these oblations which he offers, are forms of him.
</details>

##  ०२
<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

स᳘ य᳘द्यज्ञ᳘स्यार्छे᳟त् ॥  
यां तत्प्र᳘ति देव᳘तां म᳘न्येत ता᳘मनुसमी᳘क्ष्य जुहुयाद्य᳘दि दीक्षोपस᳘त्स्वाहवनी᳘ये य᳘दि प्र᳘सुतऽआ᳘ग्नीध्रे वि वा᳘ऽएत᳘द्यज्ञ᳘स्य प᳘र्व स्रᳫंसते यद्ध्व᳘लति साॗ यैव त᳘र्हि त᳘त्र देव᳘ता भ᳘वति त᳘यैॗवैत᳘द्देव᳘तया यज्ञं᳘ भिषज्य᳘ति त᳘या देवत᳘या यज्ञं᳘ प्रतिसं᳘दधाति ॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

2. If any part of the sacrifice were to fail, let him make an oblation with regard to that same deity for whom he may have intended (that part),--on the Āhavanīya, if it is during the initiation and the

 Upasads; on the Āgnīdhra, if it is at the Soma-pressing;--for whatever joint of the sacrifice fails, that breaks; and whichever then is the deity in that (part of the sacrifice) through that deity he heals the sacrifice, through that one he makes the sacrifice complete again [^egg_552].

[^egg_552]: 206:1 Cf. IV, 5, 7, 6.
</details>

##  ०३
<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

स य᳘द्येनं म᳘नसाभि᳘ध्यातः ॥  
यज्ञोॗ नोपन᳘मेत्परमेष्ठि᳘ने स्वाहे᳘ति जुहुयात्परमेष्ठी हि स त᳘र्हि भ᳘वत्य᳘प पाप्मा᳘नᳫं हतऽउ᳘पैनं यज्ञो᳘ नमति ॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

3. If, however, the sacrifice, resolved upon in his mind, were not to incline to him [^egg_553], let him perform an oblation with, 'To Parameshṭḥin hail!' for Parameshṭḥin (the most high) he (Soma [^egg_554]) then is: he repels evil, and the sacrifice inclines to him.

[^egg_553]: 206:2 That is to say, if untoward circumstances were to arise threatening to prevent the intended Soma-sacrifice. The mental resolve (saṁkalpa), on the part of the Sacrificer, is the first act in the performance of a sacrifice.

[^egg_554]: 206:3 Or, it (the sacrifice), as Harisvāmin takes it.
</details>

##  ०४
<details><summary>मूलम् - Weber</summary>

अ᳘थ य᳘द्येनं वाॗचाभिव्या᳘हृतः ॥  
यज्ञोॗ नोपन᳘मेत्प्रजा᳘पतये स्वाहे᳘ति जुहुयात्प्रजा᳘पतिर्हि स त᳘र्हि भ᳘वत्य᳘प पाप्मा᳘नᳫं हतऽउ᳘पैनं यज्ञो᳘ नमति ॥
</details>

<details><summary>Eggeling</summary>

4. And if the sacrifice, bespoken by his speech [^egg_555] were not to incline to him, let him perform an oblation with, 'To Prajāpati hail!' for Prajāpati (the lord of creatures) he then is: he repels evil, and the sacrifice inclines to him.

[^egg_555]: 206:4 That is, after he has announced his intention to perform a Soma-sacrifice, by saying 'Somena yakshye,' 'I will sacrifice by means of Soma.'
</details>

##  ०५

</details>

</details>
