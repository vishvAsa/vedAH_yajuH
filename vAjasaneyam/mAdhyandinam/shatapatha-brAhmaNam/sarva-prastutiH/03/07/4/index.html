Eggeling
  1. ‘Uniting blessings, not uniting bodies;’ whereby he means to say, ‘Unite ye your blessings only, but not also your bodies;’ for were they also to unite their bodies, Agni (the fire) would burn the sacrificer. Now when this one sacrifices in the fire, he gives gifts to Agni; and whatever blessing the priests here invoke upon the sacrificer, all that Agni accomplishes. Thus they unite only their blessings, but not also their bodies: wherefore he says, ‘Uniting blessings, not uniting bodies.’
