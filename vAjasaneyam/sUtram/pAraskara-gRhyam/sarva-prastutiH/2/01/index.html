  1. Or before the lapse of the third (year).

षोडशवर्षस्य केशान्तः ३

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. When he is sixteen years old, the Keśānta (i.e. the shaving of his beard, is to be done),

०४ यथामङ्गलं वा सर्वेषाम् ...{Loading}...

यथामङ्गलं वा सर्वेषाम् ४

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. Or, according as it is considered auspicious by all (the different families).

०५ ब्राह्मणान्भोजयित्वा माता कुमारमादायाप्लाव्याहते ...{Loading}...

ब्राह्मणान्भोजयित्वा माता कुमारमादायाप्लाव्याहते वाससी परिधाप्याङ्क आधाय पश्चादग्नेरुपविशति ५

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. After food has been distributed to the Brāhmaṇas, the mother takes the boy, bathes him, puts on him an under and an upper garment which have not yet been washed, and putting him on her lap, she sits down to the west of the fire.

०६ अन्वारब्ध आज्याहुतीर्हुत्वा प्राशनान्ते ...{Loading}...

अन्वारब्ध आज्याहुतीर्हुत्वा प्राशनान्ते शीतास्वप्सूष्णा आसिञ्चत्युष्णेन वाय उदकेनेह्यदिते केशान्वपेति ६

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. The father taking hold (of his wife) sacrifices Ājya oblations, and after he has partaken of the (sacrificial) food, he pours warm water into cold water with (the words), ‘With warm water come hither, Vāyu! Aditi, cut the hair.’

०७ केशान्वपेति ...{Loading}...

केशान्वपेति ६

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. At the Keśānta ceremony (Sūtra 3), ‘hair and beard’ (instead of ‘hair’).

०८ केशश्मश्व्रिति च केशान्ते ...{Loading}...

केशश्मश्व्रिति च केशान्ते ७

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. He throws a piece of fresh butter, or of ghee, or some curds into it (i.e. into the water, Sara 6).

०९ अथात्र नवनीतपिण्डं घृतपिण्डम् ...{Loading}...

अथात्र नवनीतपिण्डं घृतपिण्डं दध्नो वा प्रास्यति ८

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg

9 2 . Taking some (water) he moistens the hair near the right ear with (the formula), ‘On the impulse of Savitṛ may the divine waters moisten thy body in order that long life and splendour may be thine.’

१० तत आदाय दक्षिणम् ...{Loading}...

तत आदाय दक्षिणं गोदानमुन्दति । सवित्रा प्रसूता दैव्या आप उन्दन्तु ते तनूं दीर्घायुत्वाय वर्चस इति ९

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg

10 3 . Having unravelled (the hair) with a porcupine’s quill that has three white spots, he puts three young Kuśa shoots into it with (the formula), ‘Herb’ (Vāj. Saṃh. IV, 1).

११ त्र्येप्या शलल्या विनीय ...{Loading}...

त्र्येप्या शलल्या विनीय त्रीणि कुशतरुणान्यन्तर्दधात्योषध इति १०

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg

11 4 . Taking up a copper razor with (the formula), ‘Friendly by name’ (Vāj. Saṃh. III, 63 a), he cuts (the hair) with (the formula), ‘I cut off’ (ibid. 63b), (and with the formula,) ‘The razor with which Savitṛ, the knowing one, has shaven (the beard) of king Soma and Varuṇa, with that, ye Brāhmaṇas, shave his (head), in order that he may be blessed with long life and may reach old age.’

१२ शिवो नामेति लोहक्षुरमादाय ...{Loading}...

शिवो नामेति लोहक्षुरमादाय निवर्तयामीति प्रवपति येनावपत्सविता क्षुरेण सोमस्य राज्ञो वरुणस्य विद्वान् । तेन ब्रह्माणो वपतेदमस्यायुष्यञ्जरदष्टिर्यथासदिति ११

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. Cutting off (the Kuśa shoots) together with the hair, he throws them on a lump of bull’s dung which they keep northwards of the fire.

१३ सकेशानि प्रच्छिद्यानडुहे गोमयपिण्डे ...{Loading}...

सकेशानि प्रच्छिद्यानडुहे गोमयपिण्डे प्रास्यत्युत्तरतो ध्रियमाणे १२

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. In the same way two other times silently.

१४ एवं द्विरपरं तूष्णीम् ...{Loading}...

एवं द्विरपरं तूष्णीम् १३

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. The moistening and the other rites are repeated with the two other (tufts of hair).

१५ इतरयोश्चोन्दनादि ...{Loading}...

इतरयोश्चोन्दनादि १४

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. Behind with (the verse), ‘The threefold age’ (Vāj. Saṃh. III, 62).

१६ अथ पश्चात्त्र्यायुषमिति ...{Loading}...

अथ पश्चात्त्र्यायुषमिति १५

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg

16 5 . Then on the left side with (the verse), ‘With that prayer by which mayst thou, a mighty one, go to heaven, and long mayst thou see the sun: with that prayer I shave thee for the sake of life, of existence, of glory, of welfare.’

१७ अथोत्तरतो येन भूरिश्चरा ...{Loading}...

अथोत्तरतो येन भूरिश्चरा दिवं ज्योक्च पश्चाद्धि सूर्यम् । तेन ते वपामि ब्रह्मणा जीवातवे जीवनाय सुश्लोक्याय स्वस्तय इति १६

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. Three times he shaves round the head, from left to right;

१८ त्रिः क्षुरेण शिरः ...{Loading}...

त्रिः क्षुरेण शिरः प्रदक्षिणं परिहरति समुखं केशान्ते १७

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. Including the face, at the Keśānta ceremony.

१९ यत्क्षुरेण मज्जयता सुपेशसा ...{Loading}...

यत्क्षुरेण मज्जयता सुपेशसा वप्त्वा वावपति केशाञ्छिन्धि शिरो माऽस्यायुः प्रमोषीः १८

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...

19 6 . (He recites the verse,) ‘When the shaver shaves his hair with the razor, the wounding, the well-shaped, purify his head, but do not take away his life.’

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg

20 7 . He adds (the word), ‘his face’ at the Keśānta ceremony.

२१ ताभिरद्भिः शिरः समुद्य ...{Loading}...

ताभिरद्भिः शिरः समुद्य नापिताय क्षुरं प्रयच्छति । अक्षुण्वन्परिवपेति २०

Oldenberg
  1. With that water (Sūtras 6, 8) he moistens his head, and gives the razor to the barber with (the words), ‘Without wounding him, shave him.’

२२ यथामङ्गलं केशशेषकरणम् ...{Loading}...

सर्वाष् टीकाः ...{Loading}...
Oldenberg
  1. The locks of hair which are left over, are to be arranged as it is considered auspicious (in his family).

अनुगुप्तमेतं सकेशं गोमयपिण्डं निधाय गोष्ठे पल्वल उदकान्ते वाऽऽचार्याय वरं ददाति २२

संवत्सरं ब्रह्मचर्यमवपनं च केशान्ते द्वादशरात्रं षड्रात्रं त्रिरात्रमन्ततः २४

  1. After the Keśānta has been performed, (the youth) should observe chastity and should not be shaven through one year, or twelve nights, or six nights, or at least three nights.
