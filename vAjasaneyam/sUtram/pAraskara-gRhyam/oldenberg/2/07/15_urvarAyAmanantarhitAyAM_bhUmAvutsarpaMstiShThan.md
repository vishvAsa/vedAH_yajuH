15 [^8] . Let him not void urine or excrements on a ploughed field, on uncovered ground, or while rising up or standing.


[^8]:  Gautama IX, 38; Vasiṣṭha XII, 13; Āpastamba I, 30, 15. 18. Before easing himself, he shall first cover the ground with grass or the like.
