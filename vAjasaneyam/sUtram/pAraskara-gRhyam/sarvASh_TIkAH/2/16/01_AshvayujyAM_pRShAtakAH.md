1 [^1] . On the full-moon day of Āśvayuja the (offerings of) Pṛśhātakas (are made).


[^1]:  16, 1. Pṛṣātaka means a mixture of curds and butter. Comp. Śāṅkhāyana IV, 16, 3; Āśvalāyana II, 2, 3; Gṛhya-saṃgraha-pariśiṣṭa II, 59.
