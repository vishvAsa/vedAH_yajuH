## Comment
Found also (vss. 1, 2) in Pāipp. xix. Used by Kāuś. (14. 7), with i. 2, 19-21, vi. 66, 67, 97-99, in a rite for victory over enemies; belongs (note to 14.7) to the aparājita gaṇa.


## Translations
Translated: Ludwig, p. 372; Griffith, i. 281.
