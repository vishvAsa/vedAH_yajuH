## Comment
⌊Part (vs. 4) prose.⌋ Found also in Pāipp. xix. Used by Kāuś. (59. 11), in a kāmya rite, by one desiring a wife; and again, in the nuptial ceremonies (78. 10), with vi. 78 etc.
