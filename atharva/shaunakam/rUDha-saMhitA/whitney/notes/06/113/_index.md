## Comment
In Pāipp. ⌊i.⌋ is found only the first half-verse, much corrupted. It is employed by Kāuç. (46. 26) in company with the preceding hymn, which see. Verse 2 c, d is specified in the course of the rite, as accompanying the depositing of the "upper fetters" in river-foam.
