## Comment
Found also in Pāipp. xix. Used by Kāuś. (29. 28) in a remedial rite against the poison of serpents.


## Translations
Translated: Ludwig, p. 501; Florenz, 262 or 14; Griffith, i. 250; Bloomfield, 28, 461.—See Bergaigne-Henry, Manuel, p. 149.
