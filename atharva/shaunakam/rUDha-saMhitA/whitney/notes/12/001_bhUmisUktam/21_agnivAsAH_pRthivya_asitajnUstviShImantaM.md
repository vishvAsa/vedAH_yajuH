## Translation
21. Let the earth, fire-clad, black-kneed, make me sharpened, brilliant  
(*tvíṣīmant*).

## Notes
This verse is quoted by *pratīka* in GB. i. 2. 9. As to the ritual uses  
of it and of vs. 20, see the note to vs. 19. Ppp. reads *tviṣīvantaṁ* in  
b.
