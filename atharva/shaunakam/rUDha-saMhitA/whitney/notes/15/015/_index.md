## Comment
*⌊The Anukr. counts ‘sya as asya in vss. 3, 4, 7, and 8, and thus makes them count as 16, 17, 17, and 17 syllables respectively. The text says simply tisro bhurijas; but vss. 4, 7, and 8 must be meant.⌋
